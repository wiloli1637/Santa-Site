Charlie

Charlie the Elf is one of Santa's most important helpers, he controls the loading of the sleigh. He enjoys his job and has received "Elf of the Year" many times. He works hard and encourages his fellow elves to have fun while preparing for Christmas.

Alison

Alison the Elf is one of Santa's worker elves. She constructs countless toys for little boys and girls. Some days she gets distracted by playing with the toys, but Alison always makes sure she finishes the job.

Elvis

Elvis the Elf is one of Santa's jokers. Every so often he will build a toy or put a present in the sleigh. Mostly he just entertains the elves by being goofy and clumsy. He takes away the stress of working and makes time fly.
